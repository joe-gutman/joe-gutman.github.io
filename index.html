About Me

Hey, I'm Joe. I'm a self-taught programmer and designer, currently working on a Computer Science degree at Western Governors University. You might be wondering how I can be self-taught and still go to school. My interest in programming began years ago-back when I honestly didn’t think I was smart enough to be a programmer. But I had a goal that pushed me past those self-imposed limits: I wanted to make my designs interactive.

At first, I was just tearing apart scripts and mashing them together to get things working. Turns out, I really enjoyed it, so I started taking it more seriously. Over the years, I worked through a bunch of free online courses and eventually ended up in Hack Reactor's bootcamp. I went in wanting to learn full-stack development, and I came out with those skills, and a lot more. I learned to work closely with a great group of developers and even led a team of eight to build a mobile app for our final project.

The bootcamp made me realize I wanted to take programming even further, so I decided to formalize my learning with a degree at WGU. Now, I'm a junior developer looking for an internship while I finish school. If you’re looking for a motivated junior developer who’s always ready to learn and improve, I’d be happy to chat!
